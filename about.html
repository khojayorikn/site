About Me

Kom Khojayori is a current student of Virginia Commonwealth University's Graphic Design Program. He was born in Tajikistan but has lived in the United States for most of his life. Building a passion for design since he was making posters in middle school, Kom is an avid designer influenced by the world around him, drawing inspiration from the people he meets and the places he goes. He has previously worked with student organizations, non-profit organizations, and real estate companies. In his free time, Kom enjoys spending time in nature, video games, collecting playing cards, and listening to music.
